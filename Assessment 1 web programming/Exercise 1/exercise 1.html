Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

Things you need

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs - The primary protein used in this recipe
  • Soy sauce - Adds salty and umami flavor
  • White vinegar - Provides acidity and helps tenderize the chicken
  • Onion and garlic - Essential aromatics for flavor enhancement
  • Peppercorns - Adds a subtle spice and depth of flavor
  • Sugar - Balances out the acidity and enhances the taste
  • Bay leaves - Gives a unique earthy and herbal aroma
  • Green onion - Used for garnish and extra flavor

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken for at least 20 minutes or overnight to enhance flavor.
  2. Sear chicken in a hot skillet for 1 minute per side to lock in juices.
  3. Saut√© garlic and onion in the skillet until fragrant and translucent.
  4. Add reserved marinade, water, sugar, and black pepper; let it simmer for 5 minutes to develop flavors.
  5. Return chicken to skillet and simmer for 20-25 minutes, turning once for even cooking.
  6. If needed, thicken sauce separately, then coat chicken in glaze for a richer texture.
  7. Serve over rice with desired sides for a complete meal.
