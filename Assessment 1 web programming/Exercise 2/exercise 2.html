A Man Called Otto

A heartwarming story about second chances and unexpected friendships.

Official Site

Plot

Otto is a grumpy, lonely widower whose life takes an unexpected turn when a lively young family moves in next door. Through unlikely friendships, Otto finds new purpose and a reason to embrace life again.

Key Information

  • Genre: Drama, Comedy
  • Directed by: Marc Forster
  • Based on: "A Man Called Ove" by Fredrik Backman
  • Release Year: 2022
  • Distributed by: Sony Pictures

Cast & Crew

Otto Anderson: Tom Hanks

Marisol: Mariana Treviño

Tommy: Manuel Garcia-Rulfo

Sonya: Rachel Keller

Characters

Otto Anderson – A stubborn yet kind-hearted widower learning to open his heart again.

Marisol – A lively and caring neighbor who helps Otto rediscover the joys of life.

Sonya – Otto’s late wife, whose memories shape his journey.
